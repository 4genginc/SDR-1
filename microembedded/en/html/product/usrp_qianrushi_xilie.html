USRP E100

UE100-KIT Master suite includes: USRP E100 2 RF motherboard, 1 cable, USB-ADP cable, power adapter and installation accessories

The E100 USRP combines the USRP product family with the flexibility of the OMAP3 TI embedded processor. With 4 GB of flash memory space, 512 RAM MB and designed for E100 USRP, it provides new flexibility for embedded applications built in Linux images. E100 USRP is ideal for use in mobile applications, such as the use of unmanned systems and independent base stations. The E100 USRP is able to handle up to 8 MS / s in the ARM processor.

USRP E110

UE110-KIT Master suite includes: USRP E110 2 RF motherboard, 1 cable, USB-ADP cable, power adapter and installation accessories

E110 USRP is equivalent to E100 USRP, but includes a larger FPGA to allow the user to add functionality in the logic implementation. The implementation of the FPGA center can extend the processing power of 64 s MS/ in two directions, while maintaining the flexibility provided by the OMAP application processor.

USRP E310

USRP E310 KIT (2x2 MIMO, 70MHz - 6GHz)

The USRP E310 offers a portable stand-alone SDR platform designed for field deployment. The flexible 2x2 MIMO AD9361 transceiver from Analog Devices provides up to 56 MHz of instantaneous bandwidth and spans frequencies from 70 MHz – 6 GHz to cover multiple bands of interest. The baseband processor uses the Xilinx Zynq 7020 SoC to deliver FPGA accelerated computations combined with stand-alone operation enabled by a dual-core ARM CPU. Users can rapidly prototype and deploy designs for mobile and embedded applications with tight size, weight, and power requirements.
